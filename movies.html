Movie: Parasite (Gisaengchung)

Year: 2019

Director: Bong Joon Ho

Genre: Drama, Sci-Fi, Thriller

Tagline: Act like you own the place.

In a world with rich and poor people, this movie shows how a poor Korean family tries to escape their difficult life by becoming servants in a wealthy household. You will find a huge number of scenes that metaphorically demonstrate the social status of people in society. The movie succsessfully demonstrates how people cann be controlled by money and how it changes them.
